Contested street safety projects stall when the planning case isn't made clearly and publicly — and the Hopkins Street redesign in Berkeley has been stalled for nearly a decade.

This is a digital planning report. Collision records, pedestrian and bike volumes, speed data, and community workshop feedback assembled into an interactive narrative that belongs in a planning meeting and in a neighbor's living room.

Built with React, Scrollama, D3, and StreetLight Data.
